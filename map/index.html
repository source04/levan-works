Map of me

A living visualisation of what currently shapes my thinking. It gathers the subjects I’m engaged with right now — and the connections forming between them. The map is never complete and changes with my attention shifts.

You can think of it as some kind of my interpretation of now.

Inspired by Christoph Locher, as well as A Rhizomatic Map of Visual Communication, referenced by Locher as one of its conceptual sources.
